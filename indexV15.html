Welcome to Expense Tracker 👋

Your all-in-one personal finance companion — log every rupee, track budgets, analyse spending with AI, and understand your habits. Crafted by Rajendra Singh.

What Each Tab Does
➕
Add Entry
Three panels — Expense, Income, Investment. Quick Repeat chips pre-fill your most used expenses. Use ⊕ Split to divide one payment across categories. Tap 📱 to parse a bank SMS. Use ⊞ for Bulk Add — add up to 50 transactions at once.
📊
Dashboard
Analytics for any month or all time. Sub-tabs: Monthly (KPI cards, category ring, health score, combo chart) and Daily View (day-by-day bars, heatmap, habit analysis). Day-of-week chart has ‹ › week navigation.
🗂
All Transactions
Full searchable, sortable history. Filter by type, category, or month. Tap ✎ to edit, ✕ to delete. Use 🗑 Delete Filtered for bulk removal. Export as CSV.
🎯
Budget Planner
Set spending limits per category. Envelope cards with gauges and daily burn insight. Copy Last Month to save time. Compare Months for side-by-side view.
🤝
Money & Goals
IOwe — track money lent or borrowed. Goals — savings buckets with target, monthly plan, and deadline. Confetti fires when you hit a goal.
🤖
AI Insights
Ask Claude AI about your real spending data. 6 preset analyses or any custom question. Requires a free Anthropic API key.
Power Tips
⚡
Quick Add (+) — tap + bottom right for a fast expense modal.
✂️
Split expenses — enable ⊕ Split to divide one payment across categories.
👤
Person tags — tag expenses to a person (Personal, Wife, Kids…) using the chip selector in Add Expense. Manage tags in Settings → Person Tags.
🔁
Recurring transactions — set up rent, EMIs, subscriptions once. Hit Log Now when due.
📱
SMS Parser — paste any bank debit/UPI SMS to auto-detect amount, merchant and category.
🎨
Appearance — Settings → Appearance. Dark/light mode, 6 colour themes, 5 background styles, 6 heading fonts.
